About Tirumala Tirupati

Tirupati is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is the administrative headquarters of the Tirupati district.

Tirupati has one of the most visited tourist attraction as well as religious shrine and the second richest temple in the world, the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple. This temple is one of the holiest Hindu pilgrimage sites.
